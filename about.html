Who I Am

I grew up in Lagos, Nigeria a city that moves fast, lives loud, and never backs down. That same energy shaped how I think, hustle, and create.

I started out at ICRGU studying engineering and computing, then moved on to RGU to dive deep into computer science. Every project, every lesson, I bring my own energy to it.

Outside the screen, I lift, laugh, and shoot with a camera. I love capturing moments and building things, whether it's muscle or code.
